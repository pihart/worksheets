\aviinversepowerrule
\[
    \ddx\del\avianti{} = \avifn{}.

So by the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus,
\[
    \aviprob{}
    = \avibounded{}.
\]
Now we plug in our bounds:
\begin{align*}
    \avibounded{}
    &= \del{27(27)^{1/3} + (27)^-2} - \del{27(1)^{1/3} + (1)^-2} \\
    &= \del{81 + \frac{1}{729}} - \del{27 + 1} \\
    &= \boxed{53 + \frac{1}{729}}
\end{align*}
